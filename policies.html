Policies and Procedures
______________



Please go through policies and procedures of Nennu’s Dance Academy

Dress Code


Proper attire and shoes are required for all dancers at NDA. The dress code supports our mission to provide high-quality instruction; it allows more freedom of movement, ensures safety in the studio, and instructors are better able to correct alignment and muscle use tshirt over dance pants or leggings, hair completely off face and neck.

Inclement Weather

Although NDA will follow Frisco ISD for most inclement weather closings, there may be days where the weather clears or gets worse and we will make a decision based on local forecasts and other business closings. Updates regarding bad weather days may be made via our website, voicemail recording, email, and/or text.

Enrollment


All registrations must be completed through NDA’s website.

Refund/Credit on Account


Refunds are only issued when NDA removes a class from its schedule.

Withdrawals


One-month written notice is required to withdraw from class and discontinue payments. Without notice, There will be penalty of half month fee, NDA reserves the right to withdraw any customer, without refunds, at any time of the year, due to non-attendance and/or non-compliance with NDA policies.

Class Rules


* Arrive 5 minutes before class start
* Students should come to class in proper dance/workout attire such as workout pants, t-shirts, and closed toe shoes.
* Class duration is 45-60 minutes based on age group.
* Students must attend a minimum number of classes in a season to be eligible to perform at Nennu’s Dance Academy's showcases.
* Parents must strictly not to interfere with the class and will not be permitted to enter the classroom during instruction (except during recording at instructor's discretion).
* Inappropriate or rude behavior by parents or students will not be tolerated and will lead to the termination of classes
* Classes missed will not be made up, except those canceled by Nennu’s Dance Academy. Nennu’s Dance Academy reserves the right to cancel a particular class or batch at their discretion.
* Family and friends are allowed to view and record only as instructed by the teacher.
* Nennu’s Dance Academy dance class instructors, class sizes, class levels, rules and regulations are subject to change without prior notice - all decisions made by Nennu’s Dance Academy will be final & binding.
* Nennu’s Dance Academy reserves the right to terminate class membership of any student who is unable to follow the outlined policies.
* Right of admission into all dance classes is reserved by Nennu’s Dance Academy.

Safety


* Safety is of utmost concern to Nennu’s Dance Academy, all young dancers must be escorted to and from the building
* Students must inform the instructor about any past or current injuries/medical conditions. Nennu’s Dance Academy will not be held responsible for pre-existing medical conditions
* Instructor must be informed in advance if someone other than a known parent/guardian will be picking up the child
